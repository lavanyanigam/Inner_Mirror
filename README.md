# Inner Mirror

Inner Mirror is a web-based self-reflection platform designed to support mental and emotional wellbeing in an increasingly fast-paced, digitally driven world. It provides a calm, aesthetic space for mindful self-care, creative expression, and personal growth.


## Problem Statement

In todayâ€™s environment, constant academic pressure, social media comparison, and uninterrupted digital engagement often disconnect individuals from their inner selves. While many productivity and wellness applications exist, most focus on efficiency rather than emotional awareness and reflection.
Inner Mirror addresses this gap by offering a focused, distraction-free environment that encourages users to pause, reflect, and engage in intentional self-care through journaling, creativity, and mindful goal setting.


## Solution

Inner Mirror transforms everyday digital interaction into a reflective experience. By combining simplicity, visual calm, and emotional grounding, it enables users to express thoughts, explore creativity, and track personal goals in a meaningful and non-competitive way.


## Features

### Soul Scribbles
- A personal space for writing thoughts and reflections
- Encourages emotional expression and self-awareness

### Twinkle Tracker
- Goals are visualized as stars in a universe
- Upon completion, goals animate as shooting stars
- Makes goal tracking engaging and reflective rather than pressuring

### Mirror-Muse
- Interactive doodling canvas with mirror symmetry
- Promotes creativity, relaxation, and mindful focus
